Capyzoo

Capyzoo is the most beautiful zoo in this wonderful fictional city, and it allows you to bring your domestic capybara.

More information on Capyzoo

Capyzoo is the most beautiful zoo in this wonderful fictional city, nestled in the heart of lush and colorful vegetation, a true paradise for animal lovers. You can spot wolves, bears, giraffes, elephants, monkeys, various bird species, and many other animals. Additionally, the zoo features domestic capybaras that belong to the zoo and roam freely outside the enclosures. Visitors are allowed to bring their own capybara during the tour. The zoo is open daily from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, all year round.

Capyaqua

Capyaqua is the finest water park in this lovely fictional city, and it allows you to bring your domestic capybara.

More information on Capyaqua

Capyaqua is the finest water park in this lovely fictional city, with thrilling slides, soothing wave pools, and tranquil rivers. It offers an unmatched aquatic experience for all ages. What truly sets Capyaqua apart is its proud policy of allowing visitors to bring their domestic capybara along for the water adventure. Special secure areas are provided for these adorable rodents to enjoy the water fun. The water park is open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, from May 31st until September 30th.

Capyart

Capyart is a great antique car museum in this city, and it allows you to bring your domestic capybara.

More information on Capyart

Capyart is an excellent antique car museum, transporting visitors back in time with a remarkable collection of well-preserved historic vehicles. Iconic models from the past, with elegant and refined designs, are carefully displayed in captivating themed rooms. What makes the Capyart experience even more special is that it allows capybara enthusiasts to explore the museum with their adorable rodent. Additionally, this wonderful museum offers interactive exhibits on the evolution of the automotive industry. The museum is open daily from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, all year round.
