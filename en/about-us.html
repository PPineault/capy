About website

A picture of me

Hello, my name is Patrick Pineault , and I am 21 years old. Additionally, I am the creator of this website. If you want to see what I look like, check above this paragraph; there's a photo of me. This website showcases a tourist circuit of various points of interest in a place I've decided to invent, called "Ville des capybaras poilus" (Hairy Capybaras Town). I chose this theme because I found inspiration from the algorithm when browsing my Instagram. I often come across many photos and videos of capybaras. In the following sentences, I'll explain my inspirations for the three places presented on the Points of Interest page. For "Capyzoo": It's inspired by Parc Omega in Montebello, Zoo de Granby in Granby, and Parc Safari in Hemmingford. For "Capyaqua": It's influenced by Bora Parc in Valcartier and Super-Aqua Club in Pointe-Calumet. For "Capyart": It's inspired by the now-closed Museum of Domaine in Pohénégamook. Regarding the photos in the carousel, I asked my father to be the designer and photographer since he is passionate about photography and knowledgeable about the PaintShop Pro software.
